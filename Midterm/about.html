About the Land of Hats

The Land of Hats was founded by Sir Jim Hats back in 1888. He had a dream of seeing everyone in his town wearing hats and he hoped to make that dream real.

Today, we continue that dream, by helping the people of the world find the perfect hat for them. Our cunsultents work day and night to pair hats with people like you.
