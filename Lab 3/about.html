About Cats

Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don't taste sweetness. Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision and night vision are much better than that of humans. Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw).

Cats help you relax

According to studies, playing with a dog or cat can elevate levels of serotonin and dopamine, which calm and relax the mind. Cats and Health
