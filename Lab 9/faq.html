FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT FILM DEVELOPING

How do you process your film?

All color negative film is processed in a Noritsu V30, using high-quality Kodak Flexicolor chemicals. We run control strips daily. For E6 we use a four-bath process in a rotary processor. For black and white film we use Kodak Xtol, a great all-around developer that may or may not be the best option, depending on your film type. We also have Kodak HC-110.

What type of scans should I get?

If you only intend on posting your photos online, then base scans will be more than adequate. For pretty much all other purposes you will be fine with the standard scans. For prints larger than a 8 x 10, the Ultra scans are ideal, but keep in mind they are very large files and take longer to scan and send. Ultra scans will not make old film turn out any better.

Do you offer prints?

We offer 4 x 6 long lasting, photo-quality dryiink prints on both glossy and matte Noritsu paper. Base scans come free with print orders. Quality of prints will depend on scan size.

Do you develop disposable cameras?

Yes! You don't need to select anything extra. We treat all disposable / throwaway cameras as normal 35mm development.
